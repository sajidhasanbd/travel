== Description ==

Travel Agency Companion is the companion plugin for the [Travel Agency theme](https://rarathemes.com/wordpress-themes/travel-agency/?utm_source=wp.org&utm_medium=travel_agency_companion&utm_campaign=wp_profile) by [Rara Theme](https://rarathemes.com/?utm_source=wp.org&utm_medium=travel_agency_companion&utm_campaign=wp_profile). The plugin provides extra features to make travel booking websites using the Travel Agency theme and [WP Travel Engine](https://wptravelengine.com/?utm_source=wp.org&utm_medium=travel_agency_companion&utm_campaign=wp_profile) plugin.

You need to install and activate the Travel Agency theme and WP Travel Engine plugin to make the most of this plugin.

### WP TRAVEL ENGINE FEATURES

1. **Unlimited Travel Packages** — Create and sell unlimited tours with customizable tour details, galleries, itineraries, pricing, durations, trip facts, FAQs, and included/excluded costs.

2. **Advanced Trip Search and Filter** —  Help customers find the perfect trip with search and filtering options, including destination, budget, duration, activities, trip type, and more.

3. **Smart Travel Booking Process** — The trip booking process is smooth, quick and smart. Prices adjust automatically based on the number of people, and the booking form only includes essential fields, leading to faster bookings and more sales.

4. **Payment Processing** — WP Travel Engine supports a wide range of payment gateways, so your customers can book online, and you can receive payments quickly.

5. **Progress Bar** — WP Travel Engine's progress bar shows guests how close they are to booking a tour, which can increase bookings and sales.

6. **Enquiry Form** — WP Travel Engine's built-in inquiry form on the trip page makes it easy for customers to contact you, leading to more inquiries about your travel packages.

7. **Booking Email Notification** — Customize the booking confirmation email that your customers receive, and stay informed of all bookings with email notifications.

8. **Booking Details** — All booking information, including traveler details, is saved on your website so you can access it with a few clicks.

9. **Discount Coupon** — Create coupons to offer discounts to customers and encourage them to book.

10. **Create More Tabs** — Each page has four default tabs: Overview, Itinerary, Costs, and FAQs. You can easily create more tabs or change their order.

11. **Unlimited Trip Facts** — Create unlimited trip facts, such as group size, meals, accommodation, and the best time to visit, and add them to different trips.

12. **Destination Page** — Easily create destination pages to showcase all the trips you offer for a particular location, such as London, USA, or Nepal.

13. **Activities Page** — Create dedicated activity pages for hiking, sightseeing tours, mountain biking, skiing, and more so customers can easily visit your sites and book the trips that interest them.

14. **Trip Type Page** — Create trip types to categorize your trips according to their type, such as dog-friendly, child-friendly, budget travel, nature walks, and weekend trips. It allows customers to find the trips that are right for them easily.

15. **Currency** — Sell your trip packages in any currency of your choice.

### GUTENBERG BLOCKS

WP Travel Engine provides six blocks for Gutenberg in the core plugin.

The blocks included are:

* **Trips** — Display trips according to your needs (featured, latest, on sales and custom selection) with WP Travel Engine's powerful filtering and layout options.

* **Destinations** — Filter Destinations by many criteria and choose from three layouts to display them in a way that suits your branding.

* **Trip Types** — Filter Trip Types by multiple criteria and choose from three layouts to display the best for your booking site.

* **Activities** —  Filter and display your Activities in various ways with three different layouts.

* **Trip Search** — Customize your Search filter by displaying it horizontally or vertically and adding search criteria such as destination, duration, price and activities. 

* **Terms** —  Display trip categories created under Custom Filters, such as Destinations, Activities, and Trip Types.

== Installation ==


**Method 1:**

On your WordPress admin dashboard
Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
Search for 'Travel Agency Companion' and install the plugin.
Activate 'Travel Agency Companion' from your Plugins page.

**Method 2:**

Download the plugin from WordPress.org repository
On your WordPress admin dashboard, go to 'Plugins> Add New> Upload Plugin'.
Upload the downloaded plugin file (travel-agency-companion.zip) and click 'Install Now'
Activate 'Travel Agency Companion' from your Plugins page.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= WHAT WIDGETS DOES THE PLUGIN ADD? =
Travel Agency Companion adds three useful customized widgets, namely, Featured Post widget, Popular Post widget, and Recent Post widget.

= WHAT FEATURES DOES THIS PLUGIN ADD? =
Besides widgets, it adds extra options for customizing the home page of the Travel Agency Theme.

= WILL TRAVEL AGENCY COMPANION WORK ON OTHER THEMES? =
The plugin was made to work with a Travel Agency Theme made by Rara Theme. Although, the widgets could work on other themes as well.
